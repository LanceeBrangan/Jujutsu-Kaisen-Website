Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Gege Akutami. It follows Yuji Itadori, a high school student who joins a secret organization of Jujutsu Sorcerers to combat Curses, supernatural entities formed from negative human emotions.

Main Characters

Yuji Itadori

The main protagonist, a high school student who becomes a Jujutsu Sorcerer.

Megumi Fushiguro

A skilled Jujutsu Sorcerer and Yuji's close friend, known for his shikigami summoning techniques.

Nobara Kugisaki

A strong-willed Jujutsu Sorcerer who fights alongside Yuji.

Gojo Saturo

The Greatest Jujutsu Sorcerer and mentor, known for his exceptional skills and unique techniques.

Suguro Geto

A former Jujutsu Sorcerer and best friend of Gojo turned antagonist, known for his cursed spirit manipulation.

Shoko Ieiri

A skilled Jujutsu Sorcerer and doctor, known for her healing abilities and close ties to the main characters.

Nanami Kento

A former salaryman turned Jujutsu Sorcerer, known for his pragmatic approach and powerful techniques.

Choso

A powerful Cursed Spirit with a tragic past brother of Yuji, known for his unique abilities and connection to the main characters.

Sukuna

The King of Curses, a powerful and malevolent spirit that resides within Yuji Itadori, often causing chaos and destruction.

About Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen is a critically acclaimed manga and anime series that has captivated audiences worldwide. It explores themes of friendship, sacrifice, and the battle between good and evil in a supernatural setting.
